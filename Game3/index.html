Description:

A hectic, split attention game, like Diner Dash and Overcooked.

This was an individual project made using C# in Unity.

Gameplay:

The player needs to feed their kids as well as themselves while watching out for bird poop. The mechanics are about managing your attention between multiple aspects of the game while preforming a pretty simple task of moving and sorting plates of food.

Some (Semi-)Technical Details:

  • To avoid lag, I split the terrain into smaller chunks and generated these chunks in separate frames.
  • I used object pooling for the terrain chunks to minimize heap allocations.
  • I shared objects (arrays Unity needs to assign meshes new vertacies, normals, etc.) between all the terrain chunks to minimize the amount of RAM used in general.
  • I also made a simple state machine for fun, which is used by the flowers.
