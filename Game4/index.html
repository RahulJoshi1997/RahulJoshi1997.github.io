Description:

A puzzle where time moves forwards and backwards when you do. This puzzle was based on a story idea:

Someone outside our time and space lives out its lonliness, not precieved by any of us. This ghostly person often steals things people value so that others can feel its existance in some way.

This was an individual project made using Lua in Pico-8.

Gameplay:

The player is the ghost person, and their goal is to get the hat on the chair before the man picks it up. To achieve this, they need to use the fact that time moves with them only when their feet are touching the ground.

Level Design Details:

  • As seen in the second video, I wanted players to realize how restrictive coupling movement and time actually is. So I created an elevator that kept players on the second floor, and stripped them of quit a bit of agency. As players went back and forth on that elevotor, they realized the game is about ofsetting their position
